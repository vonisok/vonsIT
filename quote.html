FAQs

How fast will I get the quote?

Within one business day. If you submit before 2pm, we often reply same‑day.

What's included in a web design quote?

Our quotes cover design, development, and launch setup. Depending on your needs, we can also include branding, copywriting, SEO setup, or AI automation as add-ons.

How do payments and timelines work?

A 50% deposit starts the project, and the balance is due once your site is approved for launch. Most projects take 2–4 weeks, depending on scope and feedback speed.

Can you integrate AI tools or automations?

Absolutely. We can connect AI chatbots, content generators, workflow automations, and analytics dashboards—custom-built for your business.

Will my site be mobile-friendly and SEO-ready?

Yes. Every site we build is responsive on all devices and optimized for search engines from day one.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Yes—whether you want a full rebuild or a modern facelift, we can migrate your existing content and elevate your brand's presentation and performance.

What happens after launch?

We offer maintenance and growth plans that include backups, updates, security checks, and performance optimization—so your site keeps improving.

Do you handle hosting and domains?

Yes — we can set up SiteGround/Netlify and connect your domain, or work with your current provider.

Is there a guarantee?

If the delivered scope doesn't match the approved proposal, we fix it at our expense.
