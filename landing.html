Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with vonsIT

What platforms do you build websites on?

We primarily build custom websites using modern technologies like HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. For content management, we specialize in WordPress and can also work with other platforms based on your specific needs.

Do you provide hosting services?

Yes! We can arrange reliable, fast hosting for your website or work with your existing hosting provider. We'll recommend the best solution based on your needs and budget.

What if I need changes after the website is live?

Your package includes 30 days of optimization and support after launch. For ongoing changes beyond that, we offer flexible maintenance packages starting at $200/month.

Can you help with content and copywriting?

Absolutely! Professional copywriting is included in our package. We'll create compelling content that speaks to your audience and drives conversions.

How do you ensure my website will rank on Google?

Every website includes basic SEO setup: optimized page titles, meta descriptions, proper heading structure, fast loading speeds, and mobile responsiveness - all ranking factors Google loves.

What happens if I'm not satisfied with the design?

We include unlimited revisions during the design phase. We won't move forward until you're completely happy with the look and functionality of your website.
